Math

Place value

Place value helps us understand what each digit in a number means. In math, the position of a number tells us its value. For example, in the number 345, the 3 is in the hundreds place, the 4 is in the tens place, and the 5 is in the ones place. This means 345 is made of 300 + 40 + 5. Place value helps us read, write, and compare big numbers. It also makes it easier to add, subtract, multiply, and divide. Understanding place value is an important part of learning math.
