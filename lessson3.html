Math

Factors and Multiples

Now we learn about factors and multiples. Factors are numbers that divide evenly into another number. Multiples are numbers you get when you multiply a number by whole numbers. Understanding factors and multiples helps with division, multiplication, and problem-solving in real life.

Reading

Comparing Ideas Across Paragraphs

In reading, we practice comparing ideas across paragraphs. For example: "Paragraph 1: Lions live in groups called prides. They hunt together and share food." "Paragraph 2: Tigers are solitary hunters and live alone." Comparing shows how lions and tigers behave differently. This helps us understand patterns and make inferences.

Science

Orbits and Gravity

Planets orbit the Sun because of gravity. The Moon orbits Earth for the same reason. Gravity keeps everything moving in space predictably. Understanding orbits helps explain seasons, tides, and eclipses in a deeper way.
